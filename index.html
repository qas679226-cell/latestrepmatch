Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about scaling your sales team with Repmatch.

We typically start with 1-2 reps to dial in the conversion process and prove ROI. Once we hit your target close rate (usually 15-25% depending on your offer), we scale the team based on your lead volume. Most clients run 2-5 reps within 90 days.

We specialize in B2B SaaS, software, and high-ticket services ($3K-$50K+ contracts). Our reps have closed deals in marketing agencies, AI tools, data platforms, consulting, and enterprise software. If you have a complex sale with a defined sales process, we can help.

Most clients see their first closed deals within 21-30 days. Week 1-2 is infrastructure setup (scripts, CRM, training). Week 3-4 is when reps start taking calls. By month 2, you should be seeing consistent pipeline growth. We track leading indicators (calls booked, show rates) from day one so you know it's working before revenue hits.

We charge a one-time upfront setup fee to build your sales infrastructure (scripts, CRM workflows, rep training), plus a small monthly service fee for ongoing management and QA. Your reps work on commission-only (typically 10-20% of closed deals), so you're never paying salaries. The exact commission structure is customized during your strategy call based on your contract value and margins - designed to attract elite closers while keeping your economics profitable.

We have a 30-day performance guarantee. If your rep isn't hitting the agreed KPIs (call volume, show rates, or conversion benchmarks), we'll replace them at no cost. We also provide daily QA reviews to catch issues early. Our reps are pre-vetted with proven track records - they only eat what they kill, so they're highly motivated to perform.

We work with most major CRMs (HubSpot, Salesforce, Pipedrive, GoHighLevel). If you don't have one, we'll recommend affordable options. You'll also need basic video conferencing (Zoom/Teams) and a dialer if doing outbound. We integrate seamlessly with your existing tech stack.
